# New Bosses
New Bosses is a Minecraft Bedrock add-on that adds four new bosses, making your world more challenging.

These bosses spawn in desert, forest, taiga, and plains biomes. All of them have the ability to break blocks, so hiding behind walls is not an option to easily defeat them. When defeated, they drop their weapons or items to craft new gear, along with emeralds, diamonds, and the new Shining Gold Block.

🗡️ Netherite Curse – Plains | HP: 750 | ATK: 12 | EXP: 1200 | Big Netherite Sword

💀 Lich – Forest | HP: 750 | ATK: 12 | EXP: 1200 | Amethyst Halberd

🪨 Insane Golem – Taiga | HP: 850 | ATK: 24 | EXP: 1200

👑 King Ravager – Desert | HP: 1000 | ATK: 24 | EXP: 2400 | Royal Golden Horn (x2 = Royal Gold Sword)

This addon is optimized to run effectively on Realms and dedicated servers with minimal hardware resource consumption and its available in english and español. 🇬🇧 🇲🇽
